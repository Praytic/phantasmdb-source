What are Stats?

Stats are essential part of your Phantasm RPG character development. There are currently {{STATS_COUNT}} Stats in the game and each of them affects your hero differently. If you keep to aggressive gameplay style, you may prefer increasing offensive Stats like Agility or Sorcery. Playing more tanky-ish build leads to stacking Resistances and Evasion. You should know your strengths and weaknesses and optimize your Stats combination accordingly.

How do I increase my Stats?

The main source of Stats is items you receive after level completion. You can see Stats these items provide in the tooltip. The amount of given Stats of an item linearly depends on the item level, which means that item with lvl 20 will give twice as many stats as the same item with lvl 10. Some buffs, passive and active skills can also grant Stats. You can see the list of stats and their descriptions currently presented in the map in this page. Try clicking on Stat name to see items that increase that Stat value.
